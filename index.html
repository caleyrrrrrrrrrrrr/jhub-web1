How to Use the Rack Visualizer

  • Drag and drop components to place them.
  • Click a placed component to remove it.
  • Use the rack usage counter at the bottom to monitor capacity.

    • This concept could be further exploited by adding further features such as:

  • exporting the rack view to a file,
  • Adding a dropdown to display categories of components at a time (servers, switches, patch panels, other)
